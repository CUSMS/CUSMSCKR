Chitkara University School of Maritime Studies (CUSMS), associate institution of Chitkara University, is the first institution in the Northern States of Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and J&K to receive approval from the Directorate General of Shipping (DGS), MoS, Government of India (MTI No. 106025), for the B.Sc (Nautical Science).
CUSMS will equip students with the knowledge, skills, and a professional attitude, fostering a deep passion for a rewarding career in the Merchant Navy. The School of Maritime Studies is committed to continuously expanding its academic offerings by introducing specialised courses that align with the evolving needs of the global maritime industry. It remains dedicated to upholding its core values, holistic educational approach, and ethical foundation, ensuring the overall development of its students.

Transform Your Career
Upon completing the stipulated periods on the ship and passing competency examinations conducted by the Directorate General of Shipping, Government of India, the Junior Officer progressively advances through the ranks, eventually becoming eligible to be posted for a captain/Master position. The exposure in a merchant navy career not only enhances technical skills but also fosters personal, professional, leadership, decision-making, preparing individuals for diverse challenges at sea and beyond.

Learn The Most In Demand Skills
A Merchant Navy career is a unique blend of variety within a single domain. Throughout this journey, one gains adaptability, awareness, and professionalism while circumnavigating the globe. On board a merchant ship, an officer learns effective communication skills, develops extreme resourcefulness, and acquires a high level of discipline. This experience hones leadership abilities, enhances problem-solving skills, preparing cadets for dynamic challenges at sea.

Study Less Work More
The merchant navy, being a lucrative and adventurous career, demands a great amount of hard work, dedication, and resilience. The rigorous training imparted in the merchant navy enables deck officers to achieve a high level of expertise to work diligently over the years. Unexpected situations and emergencies provide valuable knowledge and experience in facing and dealing with them, keeping one alert, adaptable, resourceful, and well-prepared for any challenges throughout the journey.
